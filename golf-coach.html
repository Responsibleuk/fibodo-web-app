Reporting

FIBODO Reporting is included with all modules and provides unparalleled reporting on your business performance. Reports are provided in multiple formats, from simple dashboard, to detailed numerical content. Reports automatically export to CSV files making them readily accessible to main stream accounting and analytics systems. Sales reporting provides for segmentation, date ranging, including combining future and past bookings within the same report. FIBODO reports are consistent with each other and accurate, so you can rely on them to inform your business strategy.
