Smartphones

We are now in the era of the mobile device. Whilst web applications remain key tools in business management, consumers are increasing looking to engage on their mobile devices. If you don’t have a mobile APP you are making this more difficult for your consumers and increasing the risk that they will come across other service providers as they browse their social media and web search engines.

The FIBODO ecosystem is designed to create a seamless integration between your services and your clients. One of the key weapons in this armoury is the tailored APP. Having an APP allows you and your customers to personalise their experience, through setting their own preferences, recognising their location and offering customised recommendations based on their engagement.

The app allows you to communicate directly with your clients, personalising your notifications, so that they are visible, relevant and timely and avoid the risk of important messages becoming lost in a sea of marketing emails from your competitors or indeed other activities seeking to lure your customers away. Remember the ability to send relevant instant non-intrusive notifications to your customers, that they don’t need to edit or manage is attractive to your clients as well as your business.

Apps allow you to leverage other mobile device features, such as GPS, camera or contact lists, so that you can promote your business not only to them, offering for example ancillary purchase incentives when onsite, but also to promote your business to their contacts, through encouraging social media sharing (pictures, scorecards, longest drive etc…) via the APP and indeed contact referrals.

Designed to get your customers to their desired goal, making your booking experience the easiest option every time, using the functionality (tap, swipe, drag, pinch, hold…) they have come to expect from their phone, but simple don’t work the same way on a browser.

It’s a fact, typical mobile users spend more time on APPs than on web browsers (88% vs 12% - eMarketer April 2020), so why would you want to drive them elsewhere if you didn’t need to?

An APP keeps you brand and products relevant in your customers lives. It’s safe to say that the most likely way your clients will come across your brand is if it’s installed on the device that they are continually using throughout the day. A brand that they have chosen to download on their phone that only promoted your content. Ask yourself who’s interest is it in to keep you spending on Google and Facebook advertising, where you compete with many other business for the same space? The big search engines, the marketing people spending your hard earned revenues, the third party aggregators using their bigger budgets generated off the back of your customers to steal more of your business…...
